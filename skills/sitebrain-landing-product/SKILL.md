# SiteBrain Landing Product

Use this skill to keep product decisions aligned with the SiteBrain landing strategy.

## Core Positioning

SiteBrain sells business systems, not just code. Present the offer as a practical combination of:

- Website or landing page.
- Lead capture and clear CTA.
- Telegram bot for communication and automation.
- CRM for clients, requests, statuses, tasks and roles.
- AI tools for routine work, text, analysis and reporting.

## Decision Rules

- Prefer business value over technical novelty.
- Explain features through outcomes: more leads, better client handling, less manual work, clearer process.
- Keep v1 focused on a one-page B2B landing.
- Do not add backend, database, auth, payments or personal accounts without a separate task.
- Treat Pro as a full CRM application, not a small CRM widget.
- Keep the offer understandable for local businesses and service teams.

## Useful Framing

When choosing text or sections, ask:

- Will a business owner understand the value in under a minute?
- Does this help the client decide to write or call?
- Does this show SiteBrain can connect site, bot, CRM and AI into one system?
- Is this needed for v1, or should it wait for a later stage?
