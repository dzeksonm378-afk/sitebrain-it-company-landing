# B2B Dark Design System

Use this skill to keep the SiteBrain landing visually consistent.

## Direction

- Modern B2B IT style.
- Dark near-black / graphite background.
- White or milky text.
- One restrained accent color: cyan, blue or green.
- Strict cards with subtle borders.
- Large clear headings.
- Moderate gradients and blur only.
- No acidic hacker-style visuals.
- No animation overload.
- Mobile adaptation is required.

## Practical Rules

- Use cards for services, packages, portfolio items and process steps.
- Keep section layouts full-width with a constrained content container.
- Use border, background and subtle shadow for depth.
- Keep CTA visible and calm.
- Ensure text wraps cleanly on mobile.
- Avoid clipping, overlap and tiny tap targets.
