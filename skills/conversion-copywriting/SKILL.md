# Conversion Copywriting

Use this skill to write clear business-facing copy for SiteBrain.

## Voice

- Clear.
- Practical.
- Confident.
- Not overloaded with technical jargon.
- Focused on business results.

## Message Priorities

Explain how SiteBrain helps with:

- Leads and requests.
- Client communication.
- Order in client data and statuses.
- Automation of routine work.
- Time savings for owners and teams.
- Professional presentation online.

## CTA Rules

- CTA should invite the client to discuss the project.
- Avoid pressure-heavy wording.
- Prefer simple actions like "Обсудить проект", "Написать и обсудить задачу", "Посмотреть услуги".

## Editing Checklist

- Replace vague technical claims with concrete business outcomes.
- Keep paragraphs short.
- Make package descriptions easy to compare.
- Explain CRM and AI through practical scenarios.
- Keep the first screen immediately understandable.
